Research

  1. Home

Tomato Drought Resistance

Investigating physiological and genetic factors that enable tomato plants to survive and thrive under limited water conditions.

Brassica Disease Resistance

Uncovering genetic resistance to DNA viruses in Brassica species through screening and molecular analysis.

Utah Native Plant Improvement

Using mutation breeding to enhance stress tolerance and ornamental traits of native Utah species for sustainable landscaping.

Trichomes & Herbivory

Examining how chewing herbivores affect trichome development and morphology in Solanum species using microscopy and trait analysis.

Genomics & Bioinformatics

Using transcriptome analysis and comparative genomics to study plant stress responses and protozoan diversity in feline intestine.

Image Analysis

Quantifying trichome categories and density via electron microscopy and analyzing IHC images for research on tissue markers.
