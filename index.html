Plant Breeding and Genetics Lab

We study the genetics of plants to better understand disease resistance, abiotic and biotic stress responses, and apply bioinformatics to uncover key traits. Our goal is to improve crops through informed breeding strategies that support sustainable agriculture.

    About

    About Us

    We study how plants respond to stress—whether from pests, drought, or disease. By combining genetics, breeding, and bioinformatics, we uncover how plants function and use that knowledge to grow better, more resilient crops.
